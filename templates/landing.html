Capabilities

Everything your institution needs,
in one platform.

From scanning a handwritten mark sheet to surfacing patterns in past papers — AcadeX handles the full academic workflow in one place.

📷
Marks Tabulator
Photograph a handwritten or printed mark sheet. Gemini Vision reads every value and converts it into a structured Excel spreadsheet — no manual typing required.
🔮
PYQ Question Predictor
Upload past question papers for a subject. Gemini cross-references them against your syllabus and surfaces frequently recurring topics and question patterns.
📚
Reference Book Library
Curate and share recommended books per semester and subject. Teachers add resources; students access them instantly — organized by curriculum.
📝
Mock Test Generator
Students generate 5-question practice exams for any subject on demand. Answer sheets are submitted digitally for teacher review and download.
📬
Submission Management
Teachers see all student answer submissions in one dashboard, with timestamps and one-click download — no more email attachments or lost papers.
⚡
Smart OCR with Caching
Processed PDFs are cached intelligently. Re-running analysis on the same papers is near-instant — no wasted API calls, no waiting.
